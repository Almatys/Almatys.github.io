The Evolution of Game Narratives: How Video Games Have Changed Storytelling

Video games have transformed storytelling through interactivity and immersion, allowing players to influence outcomes. Titles like The Last of Us and Detroit: Become Human showcase this shift, while games like Minecraft use emergent storytelling. As VR and AR develop, video games will further redefine narrative techniques, highlighting their unique storytelling potential.

Tapped Out

In this gripping new podcast series, we dive deep into the aftermath of Hurricane Helene and its impact on Virginia Tech's campus water supply. "Tapped Out" explores the boil water advisory issued by the university, its ripple effects on students, staff, and the local community, and the science, politics, and human stories behind the disaster recovery process. From campus-wide water shortages to the challenges of restoring basic services in the wake of a natural disaster, this podcast is a must-listen for anyone.
